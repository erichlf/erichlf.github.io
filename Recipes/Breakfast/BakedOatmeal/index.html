Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 125g Rolled Oats
  • 150g Steel Cut Oats
  • 110g Sugar
  • 125ml Vegetable Oil
  • 250ml Non-dairy Milk
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 150g Frozen Berry Medley
  • 2 Flax Eggs
  • Cinnamon (to taste)
  • Brown Sugar (to taste)

Method:

  • Mix all ingredients
  • Place in a greased 8"x8" pan
  • Sprinkle cinnamon and brown sugar of oatmeal mixture
  • Let set overnight
  • Bake at 120C for 35-45 minutes
