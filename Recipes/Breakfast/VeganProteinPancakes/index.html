Vegan Protein Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 180g Rolled Oats
  • 2 Bananas
  • 200 ml Almond Milk
  • 40g Pea Protein
  • 1 T. Vinegar
  • 1 T. Baking Powder

Method:

  1. Blend in blender and then cook like a regular pancake.
