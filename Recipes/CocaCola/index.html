Coca Cola

Ingredients:

  • Fluid extract of Coca: 3 drams USP
  • Citric acid: 3 Oz
  • Caffeine: 1 Oz
  • Sugar: 30 lbs
  • Water: 2.5 Gal
  • Lime juice: 2 pints (1 Qt)
  • Vanilla: 1 Oz
  • Caramel: 1.5 Oz or more to color
  • The secret 7X flavor (use 2 Oz of flavor to 5 Gals syrup)
  • Alcohol: 8 Oz
  • Orange oil: 20 drops
  • Lemon oil: 30 drops
  • Nutmeg oil: 10 drops
  • Coriander: 5 drops
  • Neroli: 10 drops
  • Cinnamon: 10 drops
