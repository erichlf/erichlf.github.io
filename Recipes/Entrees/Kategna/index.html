Ingredients:

  • 1 piece Injera
  • 2-3 T Niter Kibbeh
  • 1/2-3/4 tsp Berber Spice

Method:

  1. Divide the injera in half or into quarters, and in a skillet, heat the pieces until they become just a little crispy (just a little!).
  2. Turn them frequently as you heat them.
  3. Melt the kibbeh in a microwave.
  4. Add the berbere to kibbeh and blend the well.
