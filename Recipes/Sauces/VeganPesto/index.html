Vegan Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C Pinenuts (soaked)
  • 2 C Basil
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 3 T Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 T Onion (minced)
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1-2 T Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Method:

  1. Blend everything until creamy.
