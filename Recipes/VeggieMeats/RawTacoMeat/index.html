Raw Taco Meat

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 C Walnuts
  • 1 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 3/4 tsp Coriander
  • 2 T Braggs
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne
  • Cilantro (to taste)

Method:

  1. Blend all ingredients in blender until desired texture is achieved.
